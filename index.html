Step-by-Step BBC iPlayer TV Linking Using bbc.com/tvcode

If you want to watch BBC iPlayer on your Smart TV, you need to link your TV with your BBC account. This is done using the TV pairing system at bbc.com/tvcode.

Don’t worry — it’s very easy and quick. Just follow these steps.

Easy steps to link your BBC iPlayer on TV:

1. Turn on your smart TV and open the BBC iPlayer app.

2. The screen will show a six-digit TV code.

3. On another device (your phone or laptop), go to the website: bbc.com/tvcode.

4. Type the code from your TV screen on this website.

5. Login to your BBC account or create one if you don’t have it.

6. After signing in, your TV will be linked to your account.

Now you can go back to your TV and start watching your favorite shows.
